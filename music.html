Music

Tetris has an abundance of soundtracks. We'll look at the most popular ones, namely those from the Game Boy and NES.

Origin

The main Tetris theme is the instrumental version of the traditional Russian folk song Korobeiniki, or "peddlers" in Russian, which was originally a poem written by Nikolay Nekrasov. Below are the songs:

Tetris: Type A has been remixed many times throughout the many iterations of the game, and has exacted its place in being the franchise's main theme.

Nintendo Composition

In both the Game Boy and NES games, Nintendo released an original composition to go with one of the alternate game modes.

Other

The NES version had a different Type A theme, yet it is very recognizable. Can you guess what it is?

The two games also had a Type C game. On the Game Boy, Type C is an arranged version of Bach's "French Suite No. 3" in F# Minor. Type C on the NES seems to also be an original composition by Nintendo.
